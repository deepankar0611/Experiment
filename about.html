About Project To-Do

Welcome to Project To-Do, your ultimate task management solution designed to help you stay organized and productive. Our app allows you to create, manage, and track your tasks with ease, ensuring you never miss an important deadline or meeting.

With features like email-verified user authentication, real-time task synchronization, and a modern, intuitive interface, Project To-Do is perfect for individuals and teams alike. Mark tasks as important, categorize them by day, and use our CRUD operations to create, read, update, and delete tasks seamlessly.

Built with Firebase for secure authentication and Firestore for real-time data storage, Project To-Do ensures your data is safe and accessible anywhere. We’re committed to helping you achieve your goals—one task at a time!
